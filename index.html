Discover the Heritage of Amritsar

Sri Harmandir Sahib (Golden Temple)

The spiritual and cultural center of the Sikh religion, this breathtaking temple is a symbol of human brotherhood and equality. Participants will be captivated by the serene Sarovar (holy tank), the stunning architecture, and the peaceful ambiance. The community kitchen (Langar) serves thousands of people daily, embodying the Sikh principle of selfless service.

Jallianwala Bagh

A poignant and powerful memorial of India's freedom struggle, Jallianwala Bagh holds immense historical significance. It is the site of a tragic massacre in 1919. A visit here offers a humbling reflection on the sacrifices made for independence and is a must-see for anyone interested in modern Indian history.

Wagah Border Ceremony

The Wagah Border, the only road crossing between India and Pakistan, is famous for its "Beating Retreat" ceremony. This daily military practice is a display of patriotism and national pride, filled with synchronized drills and passionate chants. It's a high-energy spectacle that leaves a lasting impression on all who witness it.
